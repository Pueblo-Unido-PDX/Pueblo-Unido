If Someone You Know Is Detained and Facing Removal Proceedings

It is important to consult with an attorney as soon as possible regarding your/your loved one’s case. Legal representation is the single most predictive factor in whether an immigrant will win their case against deportation and secure release from detention. Also important to be sure that you’re dealing with an accredited representative/licensed attorney with malpractice insurance and ideally registered with AILA. Beware of notarios and predatory attorneys.
